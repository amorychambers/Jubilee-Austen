Hi, I'm a developer that loves clean & elegant code.

A little bit about me.

I'm based in sunny South Wales, where I started my career in coding in between working and cooking for my family. My enthusiasm for coding began when I decided to create a personalised Choose Your Own Adventure story for a dear friend using Twine, and found that learning how to make all the different elements and choices tailor the story was immensely satisfying. After the story was complete, I found myself itching to fill my time with that same intricate, precise logic that coding demands.

In lieu of a sequel, I dived into front-end development, where I now work with passion and flexibility. In development I have found a career where I can work hard and enthusiastically, making good use of my logical mindset, communication skills, and keen eye for details. Let's share the satisfaction of writing clean, elegant code that achieves the desired outcome reliably, and achieves beautiful results.
